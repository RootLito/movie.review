My All Time favorite Movie

I Am Legend

December 14, 2007

I like this movie a lot that makes me watch it over and over again. It is more like a covid but the virus is way more dangerous which will turn you into a mutant(Dark seeker) if ever you'll get infected. A brilliant scientist named Robert Neville has survived a man-made plague that turns people into vicious mutants. With the help of his own immune blood, he searches for more potential survivors as he wanders around New York City alone. While the infected wait for Neville to make a mistake that will lead to his capture, Neville is aware of how outnumbered he is and how the odds are against him. This movie is absolutely amazing, go and check it out.

Favorite Movies

Here are some of my favorite movie

A film – also called a movie, motion picture, moving picture, picture, photoplay or flick – is a work of visual art that simulates experiences and otherwise communicates ideas, stories, perceptions, feelings, beauty, or atmosphere through the use of moving images.

Constantine

February 18, 2005

Demon hunter John Constantine (Keanu Reeves), a suicide survivor, has traveled to hell and back. He is aware that, unless he can accumulate enough good deeds to ascend God's ladder to heaven, he will travel to Satan's realm after death. Constantine is entangled in a paranormal conspiracy involving both demonic and angelic forces while assisting policewoman Angela Dodson (Rachel Weisz) in looking into her identical twin's apparent suicide.

Pirates of the Caribbean

May 20, 2011

Without a ship or crew, Captain Jack Sparrow (Johnny Depp) arrives at Port Royal in the Caribbean. The town is under attack from a pirate ship later that evening, so his timing is unfortunate. Elizabeth (Keira Knightley), the governor's daughter, is taken hostage by the pirates because she is in possession of a priceless coin that is connected to a curse that has turned the pirates into the undead. Orlando Bloom, a brave blacksmith who is in love with Elizabeth, joins forces with Sparrow to pursue the pirates.

Fast and Furious

May 7, 2013

The Rio robbery that made Dom (Vin Diesel) and Brian (Paul Walker) and their team extremely wealthy caused them to disperse over the world, but they are still forced to live as fugitives and are unable to go back to their families. Agent Hobbs (Dwayne Johnson) has been pursuing a group of deadly mercenary drivers whose second-in-command is a person Dom is familiar with. Hobbs requests assistance from Dom and his team in exchange for everyone's complete pardons because he is unable to take them down on his own.

Favorite Anime

Here are some of my favorite anime

Anime is a Japanese animation style that is produced or influenced by it. It is the Japanese term for cartoon or animation where the Japanese use the word to describe all cartoons irrespective of the nation. However, outside Japan, anime denotes animation movies that come exclusively from Japan, distinguished by blazing graphics, energetic characters, and attractive themes such as sci-fi, romance, and supernatural forces.Therefore, consider this syllogism: all anime shows are cartoons, but not all cartoons are anime.

Fullmetal Alchemist

October 4, 2003

Both the father and mother of the Elric brothers have long since deserted them. They decide to undertake an illegal human transmutation in an attempt to bring their mother back, but they lose their bodies in the process. With his only objective being to return his brother to his former state, Edward must now enlist in the military in order to obtain specific alchemical powers. But with conflict looming, it's only a matter of time before they are both compelled to consider their principles and, in the end, make a decision regarding the worth of human life.

Demon Slayer

October 16, 2020

Tanjiro Kamado's family was murdered by demons, and his lone surviving sibling, Nezuko, was changed into a demon. He swears to change her back into a human. Although it's fair to argue that Demon Slayer's plot is quite simple, this is actually the show's strongest point. The rules and stakes of the narrative were defined early and remained intact for the duration of the manga, which ended in May 2020, therefore Demon Slayer has never been particularly interested in world-building.

Attack on Titan

April 1 – June 17, 2017

A youngster named Eren Yeager resides in the town of Shiganshina, which is situated on the outermost of three circular walls that shield its residents from Titans. Two new sorts of Titans, the Colossal Titan and the Armored Titan, breach the first wall (Wall Maria) in the year 845. Eren manages to flee while his mother gets devoured by a Smiling Titan during the incident. Along with his best buddy Armin Arlert and his adopted sister Mikasa Ackerman, he swears vengeance on all Titans and enlists in the military. The Colossal Titan attacks Trost, a city on the second innermost wall, five years after Shiganshina fell (Wall Rose).

Up

May 13, 2009

A lifetime ambition of 78-year-old balloon vendor Carl Fredricksen is about to come true. He leaves his home and flies to the South American bush by tying a lot of balloons to it. But when grumpy Carl learns a young boy named Russell is a stowaway on the balloon-powered house, his greatest nightmare comes true. a Pixar cartoon.

SpongeBob

July 17, 1999

The escapades of a talking sea sponge who lives in an underwater pineapple, works at a fast food joint, and attends boating school. The narrative-driven cartoon follows the nautical and frequently absurd exploits of SpongeBob, a sea sponge with an unquenchable sense of optimism, and his aquatic companions.

Avatar

February 21, 2005

A young boy reawakens to embark on a perilous mystic quest to fulfill his destiny as the Avatar and bring peace to the world in a war-torn realm of elemental magic. The Earth Kingdom, the Fire Nation, the Air Nomads, and the Northern and Southern Water Tribes are the four elemental nations that make up the planet.
